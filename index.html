Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root. The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled,boiled or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar or raw and shredded , either alone or combined with any salad vegetable
